Horizon 2020: UK government underwrite guarantee

The UK government has underwritten the funding (opens in a new window) of Horizon 2020 projects if the UK exits the EU under certain circumstances.

This website is for UK participants who are in receipt of Horizon 2020 funding. It is also for UK participants in a Euratom Research and Training project or an ongoing Framework Programme 7 project.

Please enter your details to ensure that UK Research and Innovation:

  • has initial information about you and your project to underwrite guarantee payments if required
  • can keep you updated about what you need to do next

Please have your grant agreement to hand before you start. If you do not have a signed grant agreement yet, please return to this page when you have one.

It takes approximately 10 minutes to register one project. We are unable to save your progress so you must submit or you will need to start again. Check your details very carefully before you submit as you will not be able to make changes later.
