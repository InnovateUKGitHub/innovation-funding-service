Innovate UK has developed a range of options for supporting its existing customers with current projects through the impacts of the pandemic, including:

  • Timing extensions
  • Monthly grant payments
  • Continuity Grants
  • Innovation Continuity Loans

The following questions are designed to help you navigate through the funding support available to find the best fit option for you. Once you have identified this, we will direct you to the appropriate application form. All support is subject to status and our assessment of your application.

If you are looking for funding for a new project, support is also available for innovators pursuing new projects, information on which can be found here (opens in a new window).

Project change request

Cashflow

We may be able to provide you with monthly rather than quarterly payments to accelerate the grant towards your eligible project costs. You will still have to fund the costs not covered by your grant.

Contact your Innovate UK monitoring officer for more information.

Loan

We may be able to offer you an innovation continuity loan of between £250,000 and £1,600,000 for a period of up to 7 years, to support project costs not covered by your grant. You will have to repay the loan with interest. You must provide us with your financial information so that we can assess the continued financial viability of your business.

Apply for an innovation continuity loan (opens in a new window)

Continuity grant application

We may be able to offer you a continuity grant of up to £250,000 to support your project and reasonable business costs not covered by your grant. We will need you to provide confirmation of your eligibility. We will also take publicly available financial information into account to assess the continued financial viability of your business.
