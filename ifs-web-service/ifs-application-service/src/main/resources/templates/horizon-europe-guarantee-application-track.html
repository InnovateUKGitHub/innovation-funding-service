What happens next?

We will now review your application to ensure all the correct information has been provided. When this has been reviewed and verified the application will proceed to the project setup stage.

Verification checks

You will carry out background compliance checks on your organisation. Once these are complete, we will contact you.

Once your application is verified

You will proceed to the next stage of our process. You will be asked to set up your project and provide us with additional project contact details. We will ask further information, including:

  • re-confirmation of your project's location
  • your organisation's bank (BACS) details if you are based in the UK
  • your international bank account number (IBAN) details if you are based in a British Overseas Territory
  • a profile of your project spend

Application feedback

Since we are not assessing the research and innovation content of your application to Horizon Europe we will not provide individual feedback.

You can give feedback to help us improve this service. This should take no more than 2 minutes.
