Invitation to assess 'Technology inspired'

You are invited to act as an assessor for the competition 'Technology Inspired'. Please read the information below and confirm whether you are available to assess applications for this competition.

15 to 25 April - assessment period

This is the allocated timescale for reading, scoring, providing written comments and submitting your assessments in our online service. Each assessor could receive up to 10 proposals to assess, however this number could be less dependent on how many applications have been submitted. Our assessment fee for this competition is £100 per application.

The assessor webinar briefing is mandatory and will last one hour, taking place on 21 March.

Competition details - Technology Inspired

Innovate UK is to invest up to £3 million in feasibility studies to stimulate innovation across four enabling technology areas that underpin future UK growth: advanced materials, biosciences, electronics, sensors and photonics, and information and communication technology (ICT).

See competition brief

You should reject this competition if:

  • you are unavailable for the assessment period dates
  • you are unable to meet the assessment deadlines
  • this competition is outside your area of expertise
  • you have a direct conflict of interest, for example intending to apply to this competition yourself
