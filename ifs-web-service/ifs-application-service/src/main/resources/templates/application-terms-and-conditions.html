4. Payment of Award

4.1. The Award will be payable against the net eligible costs incurred and paid on or after the Project Start Date and on or before the Project End Date declared by the Lead Participant in the acceptance of the Award Confirmation Letter. The Award will be payable by instalments on submission by the Participants of a claim and the supporting information set out in paragraph 4.3.

4.2. Unless Innovate UK otherwise agrees, claims for costs incurred and paid must be submitted promptly on a quarterly basis commencing from the Project Start Date.

4.3. Claims must be supported by the following:

4.3.1. a current forecast as at the claim date of the Project costs to the end of the Project, detailing for the each Participant the expected costs for each future claim period, entered with the claim by each Participant and highlighting any significant variations from the last forecast; and

4.3.2. All award holders should be aware that Innovate UK operates an annual cap policy by which any underspend exceeding £5,000, relative to the projects financial forecast at the beginning of the financial year will be retained at Innovate UKs financial year end (31st March). For the avoidance of doubt this money will not be available subsequently to the project.

4.3.3. Requests for adjustments to forecasts will not be accept in quarter 4 of Innovate UKs financial year (i.e. January – March) and amendments where accepted may take up to 3 months to be reflected in the system.

4.4. When making claims pursuant to paragraph 4.1, the following applies:

4.4.1. claims must be for costs incurred and paid for wholly completed quarters only (the Participants are not allowed to submit more than one claim to Innovate UK relating to any particular calendar quarter), except for the final Quarter where the Participants may submit a claim for costs incurred but not necessarily defrayed prior to the Project End Date, provided that:

4.4.1.1. the payment is for eligible labour, overhead and/or travel and subsistence costs only;

4.4.1.2. the payment is defrayed within 30 days of the Project End Date; and

4.4.1.3. the payment is included within the final Independent Accountant’s Report.

Failure to comply with this paragraph 4.4.1 will result in Innovate UK reclaiming Award against all such costs incurred.

4.4.2. once a claim and the supporting documentation set out in paragraph 4.3 has been received by Innovate UK , the corresponding Award will normally be paid within 30 days, unless it is necessary for Innovate UK to seek further information to support the claim;

4.4.3. where Innovate UK needs to seek further information to support or understand the claim, Innovate UK agrees to contact the Participants within 14 days of the claim being received.

4.5. Innovate UK shall be under no obligation to pay any claim received by Innovate UK more than 30 days after the Quarter during which the eligible cost(s) specified in the claim was incurred and paid by the Participants;

4.6. Innovate UK shall be under no obligation to make any payment on claims received later than 3 months after completion of the Project, and there will be a general presumption that claims received after this date will not be paid;

4.7. Innovate UK reserves the right not to consider new applications from organisations who have failed to return required financial reports on any projects funded by Innovate UK.

4.8. Notwithstanding the provisions of paragraph 4, Innovate UK is under no obligation to pay more than 85% of the Award to the Participants until all outstanding claims and their supporting information, as set out in paragraph 4.3, have been received and the final Accountant’s Report has been approved, such approval not being unreasonably withheld or delayed. Failure to comply with the provision of the Accountant’s Report shall be considered to be a breach of the Agreement under paragraph 6.4.

Where the Participant is a Large Company, according to the definition provided by the European Commission, in receipt of Award of £2,000,000 (Two Million Pounds) or more, Innovate UK shall retain any cumulative unspent Award awarded to the Project for distribution to other innovation projects.
