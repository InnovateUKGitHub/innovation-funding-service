What happens next?

Assessment process

Once the submission deadline is reached and your application meets the eligibility criteria, it will be sent for assessment.

The assessment process follows these steps:

  • eligible applications will be independently evaluated by expert assessors
  • each application is evaluated by up to 3 assessors by the same set criteria
  • the expert assessors will attend the Show & Tell presentation of your Discovery Phase Project and may ask specific questions about your presentation
  • eligible applicants will be invited to a virtual interview with the panel of expert assessors, consisting of a presentation (15 mins) and Q&A session (15 mins)

Decision notification

You and your project partners will be emailed notification of the decision by .

If your application is successful

If you are successful with your application, you will be contacted by your assigned Innovation Lead and Monitoring Officer. They will arrange a project kick-off meeting and supply any additional documents required prior to project commencement.

If your application is unsuccessful

If you are unsuccessful with your application, we will give you a copy of the Recommendations Report presented to Ofgem on your project which will include the feedback from the assessment process.
