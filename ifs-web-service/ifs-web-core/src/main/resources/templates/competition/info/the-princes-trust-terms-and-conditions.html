Transfer to UK Research and Innovation

In April 2018 changes will be made to the structure of the UK's research and innovation bodies (including Innovate UK).  A new body corporate will be established under the Higher Education and Research Act 2017 that brings together the UK's 7 Research Councils, Innovate UK and a new organisation, Research England.  This new body will be known as UK Research and Innovation (UKRI).

With the establishment of UKRI, Innovate UK (formerly the Technology Strategy Board) will cease to exist as a body corporate in its own right and will become a committee or 'Council' of UKRI. Innovate UK will continue to administer contract and grant funding and to manage the relationship with MSPs as a Council of UKRI. However, existing contracts awarded by Innovate UK under the DPS will be transferred to UKRI under a statutory transfer scheme and future contracts awarded under the DPS will be entered into by UKRI. More information is available from the UKRI website.

Innovate UK will pay your allowance and grant in monthly instalments in advance, you need to submit a monthly claim which has been approved by your Innovation Champion. You need to submit your claim a month in advance. This means you need to have a clear plan of your project eligible costs a month ahead which must be agreed with your Innovation Champion. The allowance will be a set payment every month.

Your claims must include:

  • an itemised summary of your projected costs for the next month

You must keep all the originals to accompany your Statement of Expenditure which you will send to us at the end of your Project

The Innovation Champion will need to authorise your claim. Innovate UK will normally pay the claim within 10 working days of receiving this authorisation. We will not pay any claim received later than 1 month after completion of the project.
