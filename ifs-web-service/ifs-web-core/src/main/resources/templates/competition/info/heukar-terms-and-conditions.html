Any personal data that you provide to UK Research and Innovation in connection to pre-registering and then applying to the Horizon Europe UK Application Registration programme will be processed in accordance with current UK data protection legislation and the EU General Data Protection Regulations 2016 / 679 (GDPR) where appropriate.

This may also involve sharing of confidential and commercially sensitive information when you pre-register and apply.

Further information can be found in the UK Research and Innovation Privacy Notice (opens in a new window) and the supporting Innovate UK Privacy Notice and Information Management Policy (opens in a new window).
