    1. The Investor Partnerships Programme brings together Innovate UK grant funding and aligned investor equity investment into eligible organisations at an early stage to accelerate investment and support growth.

  3. OUR ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES
    1. We will run one or more competitions under the Investor Partnerships Programme with the intention of grant funding specific projects for funding applicants, when aligned with equity investment from Investor Partners.
    2. We will inform you of the relevant funding competitions prior to the start of each competition to give you the opportunity to indicate interest in becoming an investor in successful funding applicants businesses.
    3. We will also promote each competition to potential funding applicants including information on the scope and eligibility (as detailed in the Investor Partnerships Programme competition brief).
    4. We will where possible hold briefing events for funding applicants alongside Investor Partners.
    5. We will notify the funding applicants of the Investor Partners that have agreed to participate in the competition as potential investors.
    6. We will undertake the administration and management of each competition in accordance with our standard competition policies and procedures.
    7. We will allocate independent assessors to review applications for grant funding.
    8. We will review the results from the independent assessors and decide which funding applicants are suitable for grant funding.
    9. In accordance with the funding applicants’ requests, we will provide a list of successful funding applicants, along with their project and business descriptions to all the relevant potential Investor Partners.
    10. Successful funding applicants will only receive grant funding and be able to begin their project from us subject to completion of Innovate UK’s project finance and viability checks and securing equity investment in their business led by an Investor Partner.
    11. We will assign an independent monitoring officer to each project to monitor project progress and confirm eligibility of grant claims.
    12. Investor Partners will not be eligible to receive grant funding from Innovate UK under the Investor Partnerships Programme.
    13. Investor Partners will receive no reimbursement of costs in relation to the Investor Partnerships Programme unless otherwise agreed in writing by both parties.
    14. We accept no responsibility for any decision you make to invest in a successful funding applicant in the Investor Partnerships Programme and our grant award should not be considered as a recommendation from us for you to invest in any funding applicants.
    15. We give no recommendation, warranty, representation, statement or assurance of any kind (express or implied) to you as to the accuracy, reliability or completeness of any information relating to or provided by funding applicants.
  4. YOUR ROLE AND RESPONSIBILITIES AS AN INVESTOR
    1. By submitting your application, you have agreed to become an Investor Partner in the Investor Partnerships Programme.
    2. You will have the opportunity to offer investments to successful funding applicants in the Investor Partnerships Programme who have indicated interest in receiving investment from you.
    3. You agree to provide us with contact information and a public description that we can share with interested funding applicants.
    4. You agree that funding applicants will only receive grant funding and be able to begin their projects subject to receiving a minimum total investment from you or a syndicate of investors led by yourself as specified in clause 4.5.
    5. You will be required to lead aligned investment into the successful funding applicant. Aligned investment in funding applicants carrying out feasibility studies or industrial research projects must be at least the same amount as the Innovate UK grant award and equity investment in funding applicants carrying out experimental development projects must be at least twice the amount of the Innovate UK grant award. Your investment can be direct equity investment or convertible loans from either a single Investor Partner or a lead Investor Partner syndicating with other investors (from within or outside the Investor Partner pool).
    6. You warrant that you (or a relevant member of your group) has access to sufficient funds to invest in successful funding applicants to the Investor Partnerships Programme. You will immediately notify us if you no longer have sufficient funds to invest in funding applicants or if you are unable to meet your obligations.
    7. You are responsible for any decision you make to invest in a funding applicant and you will comply with your own due diligence, corporate governance and investment processes.
    8. You will review funding applicants that have been identified by us to be suitable for a grant. Within 1 month of a funding applicant contacting you, you will:

      (i) confirm to us if you (or a member of your group) wishes to pursue the opportunity to invest in any funding applicant.

      (ii) notify all the funding applicants that have expressed an interest in investment from you, whether or not you would like to enter into detailed discussions with them in relation to investing in their business.

    9. Within the timeframes specified by us and in accordance with the competition brief, if you (or a member of your group) wish to pursue an opportunity to invest in a funding applicant, you will:

      (i) notify us once you have reached heads of terms with a funding applicant and provide a summary of the proposed investment terms; and

      (ii) notify us once investment has completed and provide a summary of the final terms of the investment.

    10. You will be required to complete Annex 2 of the Grant Offer Letter that will be issued to all successful funding applicants to confirm your source of funds, any subsidy control implications and confirm that you are not connected with any persons or companies or organisations listed on the UK Government’s sanctions list:

      The UK Sanctions List - GOV.UK (www.gov.uk)

    11. Where you make an investment in a funding applicant, you must provide information on a quarterly basis following the commencement of the funding applicant’s project about any further investments into the funding applicant’s business, significant events concerning the growth of the business, any exit or listing of the business or of any business failure.
    12. Following the completion of the funding applicant’s project, you will provide information to us relating to the progress and impact of the project and the business by completing an evaluation form to assist us in measuring the impact of the grant and investment.
    13. You must ensure at all times that you act with reasonable skill and care that would be expected of an honest, fair, professional and reputable investor. Accordingly, you agree not to place undue pressure or coerce the funding applicants to agree to any detrimental terms in the investment agreement.
    14. You must provide your annual financial statements to us each year to allow us to assess your continued suitability to be an Investor Partner.
    15. You must immediately inform us of any Financial Conduct Authority penalties or fines imposed against you.
